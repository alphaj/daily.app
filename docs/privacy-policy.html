Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Daily Habit Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: We store all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with any third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Daily Habit Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) for authentication purposes
  • Habits Data: Your habits, completion records, streaks, and associated notes
  • Tasks Data: Your tasks, due dates, and completion status
  • Projects Data: Your project names and associated tasks
  • Daily Notes: Any notes you write within the app
  • Inbox Items: Brain dump thoughts and items saved for later

2. Device Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Face ID / Biometric Authentication: Used for secure app access. Biometric data is processed by your device's secure enclave and is never accessed by our app.
  • Microphone: Used only when you choose to use voice-to-text input for adding tasks, habits, or notes. Audio is processed locally and is not recorded or stored.
  • Notifications: Used to send you habit reminders and task notifications that you configure. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

  • Sensitive data (like authentication tokens) is stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
  • App data is stored in AsyncStorage on your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data is never transmitted to external servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform-native secure storage
  • The app supports biometric authentication for additional security

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device as long as you use the app. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the logout feature within the app
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Third-Party Services

Daily Habit Tracker does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage data stays on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Access all your data directly within the app
  • Modify your data at any time
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
